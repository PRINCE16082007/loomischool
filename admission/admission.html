Manage student & staff admissions from one place

  • Student Admission: For enrolling new students with academic records, guardian details, and documents.
  • Staff Admission: For adding new teaching & non-teaching staff members.

The detailed admission forms are housed in their respective modules. Responsibility for each module lies with the respective department.
